# PRODUCT_SENSE.md

## Product Job

OpenSec should reduce the owner's attention cost while increasing practical awareness of AI tooling, OpenAI updates, and meaningful developer workflows.

The product is good when it helps the user answer:

- What happened that actually matters?
- What changed about OpenAI, agents, tooling, or OSS workflows?
- Which repos deserve time this week?
- What is signal versus hype?

## What Good Looks Like

- Official sources are surfaced before commentary.
- OpenAI items are hard to miss.
- Repo Radar contains repos worth trying, not generic popularity bait.
- Korean explanations are concise, calm, and concrete.
- English product, model, API, and repo names stay intact.
- The digest tells the user what changed and why it matters now.

## Morning Versus Evening

### AM

- concise
- overnight and fresh signal only
- high confidence, low filler

### PM

- broader
- more explanatory
- includes day-level patterns and practical implications

## LLM Product Role

LLMs should improve:

- summary quality
- clarity of "why important"
- theme synthesis
- follow-up depth

LLMs should not replace:

- source discovery
- dedupe
- state tracking
- explicit ranking controls
